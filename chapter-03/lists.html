Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter
  2. Mix eggs and cream
  3. Melt butter and add cream and eggs
  4. Fold with spatula like and omelette every 20 seconds
  5. Remove when still moist. they wil continue to cook until served.
